1. Get the app URL by running these commands:
  export POD=$(kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "mysql-perf-digests-exporter.name" . }} -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward $POD 3162:3162
  curl http://127.0.0.1:3162/metrics

Probe one instance manually:
  curl "http://127.0.0.1:3162/probe?instance=instance"
